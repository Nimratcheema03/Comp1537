Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas karamchand Gandhi was born on 2 October 1869. He was an Indian lawyer,anti-colonial nationalist who started a non-violent campaign for the independence of the India.
He was born in hindu family in Gujarat. He studied law from london, and then returned back to India, but after two years when he was unable to settle his business in India , he moved to South-Africa.
Then after some years he returned back to india again, and started non-violent movement to stop the dicriminations of Indians, and to prevent them from high revenue taxes imposed by british government. he shifted his movement to SWARAJ

He used to wear simple light brown clothes as a sign of rural poor.
Also, he has done many hunger strikes. Finally, He died on 30 january 1948.

Today, each years, Gandhi's birthday is celebrated as Gandhi Jayanti. His struggles for independence can not be niglected even after several centuriesbecause he did not fought only for Indians but for other peoples also. He wanted to make indian their ruker themselves.
